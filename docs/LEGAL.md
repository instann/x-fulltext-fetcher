# Legal And Ethical Use

X Fulltext Fetcher is a read-only research utility. It should be used only on public URLs and only in ways that respect laws, platform rules, and author rights.

## Allowed Use Cases

- Personal research notes from public X/Twitter posts.
- Business and AI trend monitoring from publicly accessible links.
- Summaries, outlines, and metadata extraction.
- Archiving links you are permitted to access.
- Building a local research corpus for private analysis.

## Prohibited Use Cases

- Bypassing login, paywalls, private-account restrictions, blocks, or access controls.
- Circumventing rate limits or platform anti-abuse systems.
- Republishing full copyrighted articles without permission.
- Bulk surveillance, harassment, spam, or platform manipulation.
- Collecting sensitive personal data or private identifiers.

## Copyright Handling

The tooling can write full retrieved text to local files, but chat, README, issues, examples, and public docs should avoid reproducing long copyrighted works verbatim. Prefer:

- short excerpts,
- summaries,
- outlines,
- transformed notes,
- source links,
- metadata.

## Platform Terms

This repository does not grant permission to use X/Twitter data. Users are responsible for complying with applicable terms, API policies, local laws, and copyright obligations.

For authenticated or account-specific actions, prefer official APIs or the official X MCP flow with your own credentials.
